# 🚗 Austo Motor Company - Customer Purchase Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Austo Motor Company customer dataset to understand the factors that influence vehicle purchasing behavior.

The analysis identifies how demographic, financial, and professional characteristics affect customer preferences for different vehicle types such as **Hatchbacks, Sedans, and SUVs**.

The project includes data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, business insights, and recommendations that can help improve marketing strategies and customer segmentation.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to answer the following business questions:

- Which type of customers purchase Hatchbacks, Sedans, and SUVs?
- Does gender influence vehicle selection?
- Which profession purchases more vehicles?
- Does salary affect vehicle preference?
- Do education and marital status influence purchasing behavior?
- Does having personal or house loans impact buying decisions?
- How does family income affect vehicle choice?
- Which customer segment should the company target?

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Male customers purchase more vehicles than female customers.
- Salaried professionals represent the largest customer segment.
- Middle-income customers form the majority of buyers.
- SUV purchases increase with higher customer income.
- Married customers purchase more vehicles than single customers.
- Customers without existing loans purchase vehicles more frequently.
- Dual-income families show greater interest in premium vehicles.
- Higher education levels are associated with increased purchasing power.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Target salaried professionals with personalized campaigns.
- Promote SUVs to high-income customers.
- Offer attractive EMI and financing plans.
- Focus marketing efforts on married and dual-income families.
- Use customer segmentation for personalized advertising.
- Develop customer loyalty programs.
